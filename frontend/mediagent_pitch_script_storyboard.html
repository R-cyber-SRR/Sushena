# MediAgent: Healthcare Operations AI Agent with Compliance Guardrails ## 3-Minute Pitch Script Outline ### 0:00 - 0:45: Problem & Context (The "Pain") - **Hook:** "Every year, billions of dollars are lost in healthcare due to coding errors, claim denials, and delayed prior authorizations." - **The Challenge:** "Clinicians and administrators are drowning in complex, ever-changing ICD-10 and CPT rules. Manual review is slow, inconsistent, and prone to audit risk." - **Title Card:** "The High Cost of Inefficiency & Risk" ### 0:45 - 1:30: Solution (The "MediAgent" Reveal) - **Concept:** "Introducing MediAgent—a domain-specific AI system built specifically for healthcare operations." - **How it Works:** "MediAgent isn't just one AI. It's a coordinated team of specialized agents: a Medical Coder, a Policy Validator, and an Auth Engine—all working together within a strict compliance guardrail framework." - **Key Differentiator:** "Unlike generic AI, MediAgent provides auditable reasoning for every decision, ensuring full transparency and HIPAA-safe processing." ### 1:30 - 2:30: Demo Highlights (The "Magic") - **Step 1: Clinical Input.** "Simply paste a clinical note. MediAgent immediately goes to work." - **Step 2: Real-time Agent Visualizer.** "Watch as the Coder extracts ICD-10/CPT codes while the Policy Validator checks against CMS and payer rules in real-time." - **Step 3: Audit & Decision.** "The final result is more than just a code—it's a complete, auditable decision package with a compliance score and an expandable JSON logic log." ### 2:30 - 3:00: Impact & Vision (The "Future") - **Metrics:** "Reduce administrative overhead by 60%, cut claim denials by 40%, and ensure 100% audit readiness from day one." - **Closing:** "MediAgent: Precision AI for the backbone of healthcare." --- ## Storyboard / Screen Recording Flow 1. **Screen 1: Landing / Case Selection** - User selects 'Sample Case: Orthopedic Surgery Follow-up'. - Action: Paste clinical text into a clean input area. - Focus: Professional, clinical UI. 2. **Screen 2: Agent Visualizer (Active Processing)** - Visual: 3 agent icons (Coder, Validator, Auth Engine). - Animation: Indicators pulse as each agent 'fires'. - Status: Show status badges changing from 'Pending' to 'Passed' or 'Flagged'. 3. **Screen 3: Final Output & Audit Trail** - Visual: The 'Final Output Card' with coding recommendations and the compliance score (e.g., 98/100). - Action: User expands the 'Audit Log' to show the logic: "Rule Applied: CMS LCD L33801... Decision: Pass." - Focus: Transparency and trust.
